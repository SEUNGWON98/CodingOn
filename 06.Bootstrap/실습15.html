Lovely animals


Man is an animal that has no choice but to coexist with nature. Not only humans, but all living things on Earth should love nature. This is because all animals on the planet are using the best for free without effort or cost. We may not even know that we are using the most precious thing as a credit in some way without paying any price. The sun that gives light, the moon at night, water, air, oxygen, nature in a pleasant environment, food, etc. These are all things that you can get from nature. If any of the above is absent or contaminated, humans will not be able to live normally. Even if they live, they cannot live happily and healthily due to the consumption of polluted water, polluted air, and food. In order for mankind to continue to live on Earth, we have an obligation to preserve clean nature for our descendants.
